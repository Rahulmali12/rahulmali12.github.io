1. SETS, RELATIONS & FUNCTIONS

• Subsets = $2^n$
• Proper Subsets = $2^n - 1$
• $n(A \cup B) = n(A) + n(B) - n(A \cap B)$
TRICK: Agar Relation 'Reflexive, Symmetric aur Transitive' teeno ho, to wo "Equivalence Relation" hota hai.

2. LOGARITHM (लघुगणक)

• $\log(mn) = \log m + \log n$
• $\log(m/n) = \log m - \log n$
• $\log_a b = \frac{\log_c b}{\log_c a}$ (Base change formula)

3. COMPLEX NUMBERS

• $i = \sqrt{-1}, i^2 = -1, i^3 = -i, i^4 = 1$
• Cube roots of unity: $1, \omega, \omega^2$
• $1 + \omega + \omega^2 = 0, \omega^3 = 1$
TRICK: $(1+i)^2 = 2i$ aur $(1-i)^2 = -2i$. Ye exams mein seedha aata hai!

4. SEQUENCE & SERIES (AP, GP, HP)

• AP: $T_n = a + (n-1)d$ | $S_n = \frac{n}{2}[2a + (n-1)d]$
• GP: $T_n = ar^{n-1}$ | $S_\infty = \frac{a}{1-r}$ (agar $|r| < 1$)
Relation: $AM \ge GM \ge HM$

5. TRIGONOMETRY (Most Important)

• $\sin 2\theta = 2\sin \theta \cos \theta$
• $\cos 2\theta = 2\cos^2 \theta - 1 = 1 - 2\sin^2 \theta$
• $\tan(A+B) = \frac{\tan A + \tan B}{1 - \tan A \tan B}$
TRICK: $\sin\theta \cdot \sin(60-\theta) \cdot \sin(60+\theta) = \frac{1}{4} \sin 3\theta$

6. CALCULUS (Differentiation & Integration)

• $\frac{d}{dx}(\tan x) = \sec^2 x$
• $\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}} dx = \sin^{-1}(x/a) + C$
• $\int e^x[f(x) + f'(x)] dx = e^x f(x) + C$
• $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^x - 1}{x} = 1$

7. CONIC SECTIONS

• Parabola: $y^2 = 4ax$ (Focus: $a,0$)
• Ellipse: $\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1$ | $e = \sqrt{1 - \frac{b^2}{a^2}}$
• Hyperbola: $e = \sqrt{1 + \frac{b^2}{a^2}}$

8. PROBABILITY (प्रायिकता)

• $P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B)$
• Independent Events: $P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B)$
• Bayes' Theorem: Reverse probability find karne ke liye.
