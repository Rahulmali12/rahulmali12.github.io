1. Sets, Relations & Functions

  • $n(A \cup B) = n(A) + n(B) - n(A \cap B)$
  • Number of Subsets = $2^n$
  • Number of Relations = $2^{n \times m}$
  • De Morgan's Law: $(A \cup B)' = A' \cap B'$
TRICK: Total Proper Subsets = $2^n - 1$

2. Trigonometry (All Quadrants)

  • $\sin(A \pm B) = \sin A \cos B \pm \cos A \sin B$
  • $\cos 2\theta = \cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta = 2\cos^2 \theta - 1 = 1 - 2\sin^2 \theta$
  • $\tan(45^\circ + \theta) = \frac{1 + \tan \theta}{1 - \tan \theta}$
  • $\sin 15^\circ = \frac{\sqrt{3}-1}{2\sqrt{2}}$, $\cos 15^\circ = \frac{\sqrt{3}+1}{2\sqrt{2}}$
TRICK: Maximum Value of $a\sin\theta + b\cos\theta = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2}$

3. Complex Numbers

  • $\omega = \frac{-1 + i\sqrt{3}}{2}$, $\omega^2 = \frac{-1 - i\sqrt{3}}{2}$
  • $1 + \omega + \omega^2 = 0$ and $\omega^3 = 1$
  • Square root of $i = \pm \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(1+i)$
TRICK: $\arg(z) + \arg(\bar{z}) = 0$

4. Calculus (Diff. & Integration)

  • $\frac{d}{dx} \log(\sec x + \tan x) = \sec x$
  • $\int e^x [f(x) + f'(x)] dx = e^x f(x) + C$
  • Limit: $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1$
TRICK: Integration of $|x|$ from $-a$ to $a$ is always $a^2$.

5. 2D & 3D Geometry

  • Distance of point $(x_1, y_1)$ from $ax+by+c=0$ is $\frac{|ax_1 + by_1 + c|}{\sqrt{a^2+b^2}}$
  • Circle condition: $g^2 + f^2 - c \ge 0$
  • Eccentricity ($e$): Parabola $e=1$, Ellipse $e < 1$, Hyperbola $e > 1$.

6. Matrices & Determinants

  • $A \cdot adj(A) = |A| I$
  • $(AB)^{-1} = B^{-1} A^{-1}$
  • If $|A| = 0$, Matrix is Singular.
TRICK: $|adj(A)| = |A|^{n-1}$

7. Probability & Stats

  • $P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}$
  • Mean ($\bar{x}$) of first $n$ natural numbers = $(n+1)/2$
  • Variance of first $n$ natural numbers = $(n^2 - 1)/12$
