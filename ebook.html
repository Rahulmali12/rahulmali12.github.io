I. Algebra (बीजगणित)

1. Sets, Relations & Functions (समुच्चय और संबंध)
• \(n(A \cup B) = n(A) + n(B) - n(A \cap B)\)
• Subsets (उप-समुच्चय) की संख्या = \(2^n\)
• Proper Subsets की संख्या = \(2^n - 1\)
TRICK: Total relations from A to B = \(2^{n(A) \times n(B)}\)
2. Complex Numbers (सम्मिश्र संख्याएँ)
• \(i = \sqrt{-1}, i^2 = -1, i^3 = -i, i^4 = 1\)
• \(\omega^3 = 1, 1 + \omega + \omega^2 = 0\)
• Modulus \(|z| = \sqrt{x^2+y^2}\)
TRICK: \((1+i)^2 = 2i\) aur \((1-i)^2 = -2i\)
3. Quadratic Equations (द्विघात समीकरण)
• \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\) के मूल: \(x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{D}}{2a}\) (जहाँ \(D = b^2 - 4ac\))
• मूलों का योग (\(\alpha+\beta\)) = \(-b/a\)
• मूलों का गुणनफल (\(\alpha\beta\)) = \(c/a\)

II. Trigonometry (त्रिकोणमिति)

4. Trigonometric Functions & Inverse
• \(\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1\)
• \(\sin(A+B) = \sin A \cos B + \cos A \sin B\)
• \(\cos 2\theta = 2\cos^2 \theta - 1 = 1 - 2\sin^2 \theta\)
• \(\sin^{-1}x + \cos^{-1}x = \pi/2\)
TRICK: \(\tan(45 + \theta) = \frac{1 + \tan \theta}{1 - \tan \theta}\)

III. Calculus (कलन शास्त्र)

5. Limits & Differentiation (सीमा और अवकलन)
• \(\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1\)
• \(\frac{d}{dx}(\tan x) = \sec^2 x\)
• \(\frac{d}{dx}(\log x) = 1/x\)
TRICK: L'Hospital Rule tab lagayein jab \(\frac{0}{0}\) या \(\frac{\infty}{\infty}\) form ho.
6. Integration (समाकलन)
• \(\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C\)
• \(\int e^x [f(x) + f'(x)] dx = e^x f(x) + C\)
• \(\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}} dx = \sin^{-1}(x/a) + C\)

IV. Geometry & Vectors

7. Conic Sections (Straight Lines, Circle, Parabola, Ellipse)
• Straight Line: \(y = mx + c\)
• Parabola: \(y^2 = 4ax\) (Focus: \(a, 0\))
• Ellipse Eccentricity: \(e = \sqrt{1 - \frac{b^2}{a^2}}\)
8. Vectors & 3D Geometry
• Dot Product: \(\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = |a||b|\cos\theta\)
• Distance formula in 3D: \(d = \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2 + (y_2-y_1)^2 + (z_2-z_1)^2}\)

V. Probability & Statistics

9. Probability & Statistics (प्रायिकता)
• \(P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \cap B)\)
• Mean of 'n' natural numbers: \((n+1)/2\)
• Variance (\(n^2-1\))/12
