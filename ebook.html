Airforce Group X Mathematics

Complete Exam-Oriented Formula Book (Part 1)

1. Sets, Relations & Functions (समुच्चय और संबंध)

• कुल Subsets (उप-समुच्चय): \(2^n\)
• Proper Subsets: \(2^n - 1\)
• \(n(A \cup B) = n(A) + n(B) - n(A \cap B)\)
• Total Relations from A to B: \(2^{n(A) \times n(B)}\)
NOTE: "Empty Set" (\(\phi\)) har set ka subset hota hai.
TRICK: Agar ek relation Reflexive, Symmetric aur Transitive teeno hai, to use आंख बंद karke Equivalence Relation tick karein.

2. Complex Numbers (सम्मिश्र संख्याएँ)

• \(i = \sqrt{-1}, i^2 = -1, i^3 = -i, i^4 = 1\)
• Cube roots of unity: \(1, \omega, \omega^2\)
• \(1 + \omega + \omega^2 = 0\) aur \(\omega^3 = 1\)
• Modulus \(|z| = \sqrt{x^2+y^2}\) | Argument \(\theta = \tan^{-1}(y/x)\)
SUPER TRICK: \(\omega = \frac{-1+i\sqrt{3}}{2}\) aur \(\omega^2 = \frac{-1-i\sqrt{3}}{2}\).
Agar Exam mein \(x^2 + x + 1 = 0\) dikhe, to iske roots hamesha \(\omega, \omega^2\) hote hain.

3. Quadratic Equation (द्विघात समीकरण)

• Roots: \(x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}\)
• Sum of roots (\(\alpha+\beta\)): \(-b/a\)
• Product of roots (\(\alpha\beta\)): \(c/a\)
• Condition for Real Roots: \(D \ge 0\)
IMPORTANT: Agar roots reciprocal (व्युत्क्रम) hain, to \(a = c\) hoga.

4. Logarithm (लघुगणक)

• \(\log_a(mn) = \log_a m + \log_a n\)
• \(\log_a(m/n) = \log_a m - \log_a n\)
• \(\log_a m^n = n \log_a m\)
• \(\log_b a = \frac{\log a}{\log b}\) (Base Change Formula)
TRICK: \(\log_a a = 1\) aur \(\log_x 1 = 0\) (Hamesha yaad rakhein).

5. Trigonometry (त्रिकोणमिति)

• \(\sin(A \pm B) = \sin A \cos B \pm \cos A \sin B\)
• \(\cos(A \pm B) = \cos A \cos B \mp \sin A \sin B\)
• \(\cos 2\theta = \cos^2 \theta - \sin^2 \theta = 2\cos^2 \theta - 1 = 1 - 2\sin^2 \theta\)
• \(\sin 2\theta = 2\sin \theta \cos \theta = \frac{2\tan \theta}{1+\tan^2 \theta}\)
EXAM TRICK: \(\sin \theta \cdot \sin(60-\theta) \cdot \sin(60+\theta) = \frac{1}{4} \sin 3\theta\)
Isi tarah \(\cos\) ke liye bhi yahi rule lagta hai.
NOTE: Maximum value of \(a\sin\theta + b\cos\theta\) is \(\sqrt{a^2+b^2}\).
